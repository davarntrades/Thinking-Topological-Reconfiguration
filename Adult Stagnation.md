These describe symptoms. They do not describe the geometry.

The Morrison Framework™ gives the first precise mathematical account of what stagnation actually is — what it looks like from the inside, why it is invisible to the person experiencing it, and why it is not a character failure but a geometric condition.


This is not laziness. This is not fear. This is a geometric event — measurable, formalised, and inevitable without deliberate architectural intervention.
